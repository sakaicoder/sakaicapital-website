ABOUT US

Sakai Capital is a private investment firm dedicated to acquiring and operating one exceptional mid-sized company in Mexico. We take a long-term, hands-on approach to ownership—committed to accelerating growth while honoring the legacy of the businesses we invest in. Our goal is to create enduring value for employees, customers, and stakeholders alike.

WHO WE ARE

We are a team of passionate entrepreneurs and operators, backed by a network of world-class global investors with deep expertise in Mexico and emerging markets. Our experience spans founding, scaling, and transforming companies across industries. We bring not just capital, but the operational commitment and strategic guidance needed to help businesses thrive.

The firm is led by two managing partners with a proven track record Adela Kably and Giancarlo Solorzano, with the backing and close support of over ten renowned investors spanning six countries.
