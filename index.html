ABOUT US

Sakai Capital is a private investment firm dedicated to acquiring and operating one exceptional mid-sized company in Mexico. We take a long-term, hands-on approach to ownership—committed to accelerating growth while honoring the legacy of the businesses we invest in. Our goal is to create enduring value for employees, customers, and stakeholders alike.

WHO WE ARE

We are a team of passionate entrepreneurs and operators, backed by a network of world-class global investors with deep expertise in Mexico and emerging markets. Our experience spans founding, scaling, and transforming companies across industries. We bring not just capital, but the operational commitment and strategic guidance needed to help businesses thrive.

WHAT WE SEEK

We are seeking a profitable, mid-sized business in Mexico with annual revenues over MXN$200 million. Ideal candidates operate in large and growing markets, have a recurring and predictable revenue model, operating margins above 15%, and strong growth potential. We aim to acquire a majority stake and dedicate ourselves full-time to the company’s future success.

WHAT WE OFFER

Sakai Capital offers a tailored succession solution for business owners ready to transition their company into experienced, trustworthy hands—whether stepping back, retiring, or planning for the next phase of growth. We provide speed, flexibility, and hands-on leadership from day one, along with the support of global investors. Our promise: preserve what makes the business great, while building a stronger, more scalable future.
