ABOUT US

Sakai Capital is a private investment firm focused on acquiring an exceptional mid-sized company in Mexico, with the goal of creating long-term value while preserving its legacy and accelerating its growth.

WHO WE ARE

We are a team of passionate entrepreneurs backed by world-class investors with deep expertise in Mexico and around the globe. With proven success in building and scaling businesses, we are focused on acquiring a single outstanding mid-sized company in Mexico. We take a hands-on, long-term approach to unlock its full potential—preserving its legacy while accelerating growth and creating lasting value.

WHAT WE SEEK?

We are looking to acquire a Mexican business with annual revenues of over MXN$200 million, operating in a large and growing market with a consistent track record of profitability. Ideal candidates will have recurring, stable, and predictable revenues, an operating margin above 15%, and clear potential for continued growth.

Our aim is to acquire a majority stake and dedicate ourselves full-time to providing hands-on business, operational, and financial leadership. Backed by experienced global investors, we combine an entrepreneurial mindset with deep local expertise to help the company reach its full potential—while preserving the legacy its founders have built.

WHAT WE OFFER

At Sakai Capital, we are experienced entrepreneurs and operators with deep investing and value-creation expertise, seeking to partner with a single, growth-oriented mid-sized business in Mexico. We offer a unique succession solution for business owners looking to transition their company into capable, committed hands—whether to retire, step back, or take the business to its next level.

We move quickly and bring hands-on leadership from day one. Our team is backed by global investors with strong track records and sector expertise, allowing us to provide both immediate liquidity and long-term strategic guidance. We are fully dedicated to preserving the company’s legacy while driving continued growth, empowering its people, and securing a stable and thriving future for its employees, customers, and stakeholders.
